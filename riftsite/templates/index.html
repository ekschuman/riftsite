What is Rift?

Rift is a larp set in a medieval fantasy world in which the player will explore the land rediscovering lost treasures, assisting its people in political and economical ventures and of course protecting the realm from terrible dangers.


What makes it special?

Rift is made by the players for the players. What this means is that the world and lore are set in such a way that the players are really who create the world. Players will discover areas, trails, and creatures, and it's the players who name them and give them a purpose.


Do you have props?

Rift has many items provided for you to use on your adventures. It's the little details that bring the world to life.


What can my character be?

In short, anything. Without races, classes, professions or anything else defining what you can and can not do how you play the game is without limits. You create your own back story of who and what you were before you came here. You can be yourself if you want to.


I don't like combat.

That's perfectly ok. Rift is a role play heavy larp, cheeky humor is at a minimum and serious role play is the center of this game. Politics and sustaining the economy is an important factor and will need constant tending to keep things afloat.


What happens if you die?

Death is a very real concept in Rift. When you die you will have 1 minute to be restored before your death can not be undone. Upon death you lose recent memories, experience and learned skills. Once you have died 6 times on your 7th your character's death is permanent, and you must create a new character.
