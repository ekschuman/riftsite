Skills and Spells

Swords

Fast and agile, they fare well against lightly armoured opponents.

Skills:

  • Deep Cut -- +1 damage on a slashing attack.
  • Deep Thrust -- +1 damage on stab attack.
  • Break -- +1 break damage to shield.
  • Parry -- +1 damage on counter using a parry.

Axes

Require skill to wield and those capable will find disarming others easy.

Skills:

  • Hand Shock -- Hit on weapon causes 2-second recoil.
  • Disarm -- Weapon or shield caught in beard of axe is disarmed.
  • Deep Cut -- +1 on slash attack.
  • Shatter -- +1 damage to armour.

Blunt

Very slow but can break shields, armour and even bone.

Skills:

  • Handshock -- Hit on weapon causes 2 second recoil.
  • Break -- +1 damage to shield.
  • Shatter -- +1 damage on armour.
  • Bone Break -- Hit to limb without armour causes limb to break and be unuseable.

Great Weapon

  • Stagger -- any contact causes 2 second recoil.

Bow

If you can place the shot you will shift skirmishes quickly with a bow. Skills add an additional effect to the damage dealt.

Skills:

  • Deep Impact -- +1 damage.
  • Penetrating Shot -- 1 break/shatter damage on armour or shield. (Heavy bow exclusive)
  • Crippling Shot -- Hit on limb makes it unuseable for 5 seconds.
  • Staggering Shot -- Stagger for 2 seconds.
  • Sneak Shot -- +2 damage on hit if it is successful.

Pole Arm

Pole arms have the same skills as other melee weapons but with much more reach. They may also be up to 2 types at once as long as they have the physical representation of the type of weapon skills you are wanting to use.

Shield

Shields will disperse spells and block physical damage.

Skills

  • Riposte -- +1 damage on counter attack after a riposte. (buckler and shield exclusive)
  • Fortress Stance -- your Greatshield doesn’t break down, you can not use skills or move in fortress stance. (Greatshield exclusive)
  • Bash -- Hitting shield to shield staggers for 2 seconds.
  • [Group Skill] Phalanx -- Shields do not break down, must hold a line and can not run or use other skills. (requires 3 people with shields)

Rod

Though not capable of using skills, spells requiring 1 hand can be cast through a rod.

Staff

Not capable of using skills, but any spell may be cast through a staff. However, if the spell requires both hands you still need both hands on the staff.

Spells

Magic is broken into 3 categories: Miracles, Sorceries, and Elemental Magic.
Each has exclusive spells to master. Spells do not pass through shields but do pass through armour, and a hit on a weapon counts.
Sorceries require Intelligence to cast and Miracles require Faith. Elemental Magic can be cast by either.

Sorceries

Sorceries are learned through the Academy, and require Intelligence to progress.

Tier 1

  • Mana bolt -- 1 damage, free to cast.
  • Lock -- Stuck 5 seconds. 1 mana cast.

Tier 2

  • -- 2 mana to cast.
  • Silence -- player can not speak for 5 seconds.
  • Chameleon -- While immobile invisible for 10 seconds.

Tier 3

  • -- 3 mana to cast.
  • Flare -- Blinds all facing you for 10 seconds within 10 feet.
  • Conjure -- Conjure weapon, mana doesn’t regenerate from cast until banished.

Tier 4

  • Mana Siphon -- Drain 1 mana per second on touch.
  • Soul Siphon -- Drain 1 HP per second on touch.

Tier 5

  • Exchange -- Change HP to MP. (1 HP = 2 mana)
  • Raise -- Raise creature to health equivalent to mana spent up to creature maximum health, mana does not replenish until creature's death or is banished.

Miracles

Miracles are learned through the Church, and require Faith to progress.

Tier 1

  • -- 1 mana to cast.
  • Mana bolt -- 1 damage, free to cast.
  • Conjure Familiar- Familiar used to scout ahead.

Tier 2

  • -- 2 mana to cast.
  • Pacifism -- Hit player stops fighting for 10 seconds or until attacked.
  • Weapon Change -- Change weapon type, does not change class.

Tier 3

  • -- 3 mana to cast.
  • Magical Wall -- Dispels magic passing through wall for 10 seconds.
  • Mana Bomb -- Recoil 2x Mana spent in 10 feet radius.

Tier 4

  • -- 4 mana to cast.
  • Healing Hands -- Heal up to 5 Hp per 5 minutes.
  • Ward -- Immune to magic for 10 seconds.

Tier 5

  • -- 5 mana to cast.
  • Armoured Aura -- +2 AP(does not stack)
  • Mimic -- mimics 1 spell per quest, at same mana cost.

Elemental Magic

Elemental Magic is learned through the Church or the Academy, using the higher of your Faith or Intellignce.

Tier 1

  • -- 1 mana to cast.
  • Firebolt -- 2 damage.
  • Ice bolt -- 1 damage, target can not run for 5 seconds.
  • Lightning bolt -- 1 damage -1 mana on target.

Tier 2

  • -- 2 mana to cast.
  • Dispel -- Dispels effect on touch (2 MP to cast)
  • Turn to stone -- Immobile and invincible for 5 seconds (2 MP to cast)
  • Freeze -- Frozen 5 seconds or until hit(2 MP to cast)

Tier 3

  • -- 3 mana to cast.
  • Whirlwind -- Push 5, if collision with object or another creature or person 2 damage to target.
  • Storm -- Creates a 20 foot wide storm that blinds all inside that can't be seen through. Storm lasts 30 seconds.
  • Fire Wall -- 2 damage to all that pass through wall for 10 seconds.
  • Ice Wall -- A wall of ice that nothing can pass through for 10 seconds.

Tier 4

  • -- 4 mana to cast.
  • Lightning Spear -- 2 damage -2 mana to target.
  • Ice Spear -- 2 damage and target is frozen for 5 seconds.
  • Fire Spear --4 damage.

  • Summon Lightning Atronach -- Summon atronach with 5 HP, can cast Lightning bolt equal to mana used to cast atronach -2.(e.g if you use 5 mana to cast the atronach it can cast 3 times) takes 30 seconds to summon atronach, only 1 atronach can be summoned at a time.
